About Us


We are a community of women who seek to support each other in successfully completing college and entering the workforce. We recognize that a significant hurdle many women face is finding affordable, reliable child care while attending classes and working. We help each other overcome this by providing child care services to each other in exchange for credits earned either through cash payments or valuable community-oriented services.
